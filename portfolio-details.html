Hospital Information System

NWAFH- HIS incorporates an integrated computerized clinical information system for improved hospital administration and patient health of Military Hospital under MOD, with the objective of streamlining the treatment low of a patient in the hospital, while allowing doctors and other staff to perform to their peak ability, in an optimized and efficient manner. It also provides an accurate and electronically stored medical record of the patient.

  • Rating:
  • Client:North West Armed Forces Hospital Administration
  • Websitenwafh.med.sa
  • From:May 2011 To June 2018
  • Role:Programmer\Developer
  • Tools:ASP.NET, C#, SQL Server 2008,2014,2017

Intelligent Hospital Management System is a complete package one needs for a hospital to deal with all the day to day operations taking place. The program can look after in patients, OPD patients, records, database treatments, status illness and billings. It also maintains there in hospital info such as ward id, Doctor in Charge and Department administering.

Core module involves

Patient Registration, Outpatient Management, Appointments, Inpatient Management, Nursing Administration, Pharmacy Management, Medical Records, Chart tracking, Lab Viewer, and Messaging through HL7 interface.
